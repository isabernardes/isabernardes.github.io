Protecting the past for the future

Aegis Heritage Institute


Aegis Heritage Institute is a non-profit organisation with the aim to safeguard endangered cultural heritage and promote community involvement. We want to provide digital tools, databases of cultural assets and develop non-invasive techniques to help to raise awareness of endangered cultural heritage, which has been increasingly threaten due to war, neglect and vandalism.

Projects

In order to ensure the protection of invaluable historical and cultural artefacts, the use of new technologies will ensure their place in the public record. Non-invasive archaeological field techniques will be promoted in order to learn about history without destroying it and host events to inform and educate people as to how they can contribute to the preservation of history.

Who are we



Heritage Safeguard is a multinational, interdisciplinary team consisting of people committed to preserving at risk Cultural Heritage. We aim to develop and use of new technologies to empower local communities to create historical repositories of cultural traditions. Our Team consists of experts in various fields such as archaeology, techniques in conservation, restauration, technology and human rights.
